Cursed Princess Club

by LambCat

Genre: Comedy
Status: Completed

Summary: "Meet Gwendolyn – living proof that princesses don’t always have it all. See, although she lives in a castle and her father is the King, Gwendolyn isn’t like a movie princess, or even a fairly-tale princess. She’s got a big heart, but isn’t particularly attractive and doesn’t have a Disney-quality singing voice.

But one night, she accidentally stumbles upon the twisted world of the Cursed Princess Club, and her life will never be the same. Hexed and cast out, the ladies of the club are just the people Gwendolyn needs to show her that just because she doesn’t “fit the mold” does not mean she’s any less of a princess."

My ramblings: Cursed Princess Club is an oldie but a goodie! The first chapter released in 2019 and it just recently wrapped up earlier this year.

The story was very impactful and I *really* felt for the characters when they were going through it. This is a story of learning self-love, the importance of family, and acceptance.

I have definitely cried a couple times reading this ( ˘•̥ _•̥ ˘ )
If comedy, then why tears? ( ˘•̥ _•̥ ˘ )

Daybreak

by Moosopp

Genre: Slice of life
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "Marcus has a crush, and Cog is completely unprepared for the hijinks that follow. Hopefully in their efforts to grow closer, they can brighten each other's day."

My ramblings: Daybreak is a romance slice of life featuring Cog and Marcus. You follow their day to day life from school and onto college and you see their relationship grow over time. It's not happy go lucky *all the time*, but I enjoy reading about Marcus' and Cog's cute moments together.

Also, I LOVE the art style so much. Moosopp draws the most stylish outfits, they're always so cute :3

DeathSitter

by Puppetology

Genre: Horror / Comedy
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "A deadbeat drug addict with seemingly supernatural luck gets a job babysitting the daughter of the city's local grim reaper.
(Series will contain strong language, drug use and violence with

Homesick

by Ms.Freaky

Genre: Thriller
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "Rayne woke up with no memories of the apocalypse. Samael is just... a delinquent, who happened to find Rayne at a rooftop. They both ended up making a deal in order to survive in a world where cannibalistic monsters roam."

My ramblings: I really love the story / world building in this webtoon. The MC's past is shrouded in mystery and we see her essentially rebuild herself from the ground up in the apocalyptic world.

There are these creatures called Murks that will completely take over your body if you look at them and sooner or later, the host will die if it doesn't feed on human flesh. Often times the "infected" are no longer their previous self and is now just goes around feeding on people.

However, there are some exeptions like our MC here, Rayne. Rayne and her murk have created a mutual relationship, even though she lost her memories as a result of being infected, they both work togther to try and survive. She also meets other survivors who become allies, and even someone who knew Rayne before she became infected.

It's a really good read. It's definitely in my top 5 for thriller webtoons (⌒ω⌒)

Hooky

by Míriam Bonastre Tur

Genre: Fantasy
Status: Completed

Summary: "Dani and Dorian have missed the bus of the school of magic. Now, they must find someone who teach them how to be a great and good witches... Or maybe not.​"

My ramblings: Imagine missing the bus on the first day of school and deciding to hide it from your family and you end up going down this path of adventure and tragedy. Yeah this is what this webtoon is about.

These twins, Dani and Dorian, are the children of one of the most powerful witch families and are very gifted. The life their parents had planned for them starts to fall apart once they miss their first day of school. They end up taking an apprenticeship with the local hermit wizard and end up getting involved in dark things they weren't supposed to.

It was such a lovely read. We get to see Dani and Dorian grow up and all their experiences as well. It also has such a beautiful ending. You also get to see the author's art style develop over time because this webtoon started in *looks at my notes* 2015 and ended in 2020!

It's Mine

by LuckS

Genre: Thriller
Status: Completed

Summary: There’s nothing that Yohan Do doesn’t know about his crush, Dajeong. Where she sits in class, works after school... and even where she lives. But unable to approach her, he watches her every move from afar and will do anything to protect her. Anything."

My ramblings: So Yohan is essentially Dajeong's stalker.. He knows her favorite color, favorite food, favorite place to eat, and so much other stuff. But, as creepy as this sounds, he still tries to watch from a distance and protect her whenever she's in trouble. He has dedicated his whole life to protecting Dajeong and learning everything there is to know about her.

Truths begin to be revealed when he discovers her being attacked late at night and he has to step in to save her. She wanted to know who he was but he ended up running away. Later on in the story, there's this crazy twist that happens that puts his actions and this whole story in a whole different perspective!

Writing this is making me wanna reread it again ( ´ ▽ ` )
Such a fun read.

Love Me to Death

by Toonimated

Genre: Fantasy
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "A lonely necromancer named Victor is hired to resurrect a wealthy man’s bride. Victor accepts, hoping to get the job done quickly, only to find himself magically entangled with the bride and groom and caught in the middle of more romance than he knows what to do with."

My ramblings: As you read in the lil synopsis there, Victor was tasked with bringing Enrique's wife, Mercedes back to life after she got poisoned days before her wedding day. The ritual to bring her back to life ends up going wrong because he didn't have the full instructions. This resulted in her lifeforce being bound to Victor's and they can't be to far from each other because of this reason.

Also her memories were wiped as well.

So they all band together to recover Mercedes' memories and try to escape the city. There's also some romance here n there in the beginning but becomes really important later on in the story :)

The Mafia Nanny

by Violet Matter and sh00

Genre: Romance
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "Being an Elite Nanny is simple: protect the charge, obey the principle, and don’t get emotionally attached. Easier said than done when Davina’s first client is a dangerously compelling Venetian underboss who’s determined to get under her skin. What he wants from her, she can’t imagine, but Gabriel Angelini drags her back into the glittering world of organized crime she thought she’d left behind forever. She might get answers about her past if she digs deep enough, but can she protect Mikey from his father’s ambitions and keep her heart from getting tangled up in their treacherous web?"

My ramblings: Another BANGER of a webtoon. I know the genre is romance but it's more like a romance / action! Davina is a trained super nanny who was tasked to protect Gabriel's son Mikey who just so happens to be a part of a mafia family.

Davina tried to not get involved in their family matters but she has not choice when Mikey's safety comes into concern. Eventually, she ends up helping and getting closer to Gabriel as a result. But she also realizes that things may not be what they seem in the Angelini family.

The family as a whole reeks of distrust, lies, and deceite. So it's up to her and Gabriel to work together to protect themselves and the ones they hold close.

(Spoiler: It's each other, they hold each other closely lol)

School Bus Graveyard

by Red

Genre: Thriller
Status: Ongoing

Summary: "Ashlyn is a loner in high school with no friends. But when a fateful visit to a haunted house causes her and her classmates to see monsters at night, she'll be forced to forge bonds to survive."

My ramblings: Ah yes, another one of my favorite horror / thriller webtoons! Ashlyn and her friends end up getting sent to this kind of shadow world every night at 12pm after entering this "haunted house" on a field trip. In this shadow realm, there are monsters out to get them.

They all have to learn to survive in this new world every night and find a way to make it stop happening. So yeah, lotta heart wrenching stuff happening in this webtoon. Lotta near-death experiences.

Stagtown

by Punko

Genre: Horror
Status: Completed

Summary: "Something is wrong with the town Frankie grew up in. Hidden away from the world on the other side of an old stone bridge, the people of Stagtown spend every day trying not to see the strange things that go on around them."

My ramblings: Stagtown was SUCH a fun read. I really like a lot thriller / horror comics and this one is definitely in my top 3. The suspense and plot twists (oh boy the *plot twists*) had me hooked on the story.

The characters are written very well too! They all feel like real people in this crazy situation. Punko's use of color is also something of note. A lot of the colors in the background, characters, etc are some variation of black, grey, or white.
So when she uses some pops of color, you know something is about to go down.
