"What I've Been Up 2"

Date: Oct 2, 2024
Mood: Chill
Time: 12:33pm
Listening to: Night in the Woods OST

Happy Autumn & spooky month!

I'm back from my coding break :D
I took a month away from coding stuff since the last thing I did was rushed and kinda burnt me out. But I'm good now! Got some new ideas to implement and everything >:3

One of the first things I did is make an RSS feed! I never really liked how you need a neocities account to view ppl's activity feeds. What if you don't want to make an account? Then you're forced to choose having this account that will probably only be used to view updates or just have to check back every once and a while to see if things have changed. I might be exaggerating but yeah.

I think everyone should make one tbh. It's pretty easy to make and simple to follow your favorite websites (or other things!)

I'm participating in another October art prompt thing this month! It's bweird's OC-tober. There's no pressure to complete all 31 prompts in this one, only 10 prompts are "mandatory." And all of the prompts involve your OCs! All creative forms are welcomed including writing. You don't have to make new content for it either. It's encouraged to post old art to put your stuff out there :3

I'll be posting my entries on my Tumblr if you're interested in seeing my artwork haha. I also ramble about me struggling with HTML sometimes too.

Uhh let's see what else... Oh I started meditating recently because why not. Well, not really "why not," I wanted to try and give my brain a break from scrolling youtube and social media and ground myself more. It's been nice to disconnect from all the things I've been doing and reconnect with myself yknow. And it's not too long either, just like 5 mins a day. Sometimes 10 if I want to meditate before bed.

I'm also taking my driver's test this month! :DDD
So proud of me <3

Driving is kinda nerve racking for me but I guess that's just a part of being a new driver. But from what my family members have said, I drive pretty well. I just need to stay consistant with my practicing (I haven't) and get my ass up and go do it lmao.

Anyway, that's what I've been up to. Been trying to stay proactive and do stuff. Expect more updates more frequently again! And follow my RSS feed if you want updates outside of neocities. There's a lil browser extension you can get too (all this info is on the homepage btw).

Lock the fuck in because this is your month!
You can do it!
-Starry (b ᵔ▽ᵔ)b
