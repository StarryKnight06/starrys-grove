Anime Webtoons Videogames Misc.

Here's a showcase of characters that I like a lot. They're like mini web shrines for a bunch of different characters. I've put them into their respective groups above, just click a link to see more.

There's a lot of text on this page also! It's just me yapping about the characters, you don't have to read it all haha (￣▽￣*)ゞ
I will avoid talking about major spoilers but there may be minor spoilers like if a character doesn't appear till later in the series or something.

Anime Characters

【 Doma 】

Series: Demon Slayer
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: If I met him irl, I would not survive the encounter!

I don't know too much about Doma because he's only had a couple minutes of screen-time in the anime BUT. I do know that he is a very dangerous individual. He is second rank in Muzan's group of the most powerful demons and apparently, did something to jump to the second rank quickly. I'm not sure what that is though... I'm sure he's gonna do something horrible in the next arc and I'll be in pain ( ; ω ; )

【 Antarcticite 】

Series: Land of the Lustrous
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: Only gem that work's in the winter because everyone else goes into hibernation

Antarc is a unique gem amongst the others. They stay in liquid form year-round until the temperature begins to drop and they re-crystalize. Although they have a hardness of 3, their hardness increases in colder temperatures.

【 Padparadscha 】

Series: Land of the Lustrous
Heart Rating: B
Fun Fact: Born with an incomplete torso which leaves them unconscious most of the time

Padpa is the second hardest of all the gems but as I said above, they aren't active usually. Rutile, the doctor of the group, has dedicated a lot of their time to finding a solution to Padpa's condition. However, Rutile is only ever able to keep Padpa awake for short bursts of time. We only see them awake once, but in that short amount of time, Padparadscha quickly became one of my favorites.

Webtoon Characters

【 Annabel Lee 】

Series: Nevermore
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: She is doomed by the narrative

Annabel Lee will do anything evil or manipulative for the benefit of herself and her love interest, Lenore. Although, she and the others are competing to get a second chance at life, what's the point if you have to sacrifice your humanity along the way. She sees the whole thing as a game to be won or lost (which yeah, kinda is). I still think she's pretty tho and I like that she's a complex character.

【 Kousuke Hirahara 】

Series: I Love Yoo
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: He carries makeup remover spray on him to deter women who try to hit on him at work lol

Kousuke is the "second in line" in the family to take over the family buisness. He also thinks he runs everything or is in control of everything when he's really not. He's kind of an asshole lol (especially to his half brother Yeong-Gi) but in recent chapters he starts learning how to be a decent human being. He's also having a crisis in the webtoon rn because his mother is EVILLLL (￢_￢;)

【 Lenore 】

Series: Nevermore
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: She is 6'0!! Tall woman! Also doomed by the narrative lol

She is also trying to survive Nevermore with Annabel Lee. They ended up splitting off into two different groups and neither of their squad knows they are fond of each other. She has such a charming personality, I would love to be her friend. Also, I need to steal her outfit style. The goth macabre queen ♡

【 Yeong-Gi 】

Series: I Love Yoo
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: Yeong-Gi is his nickname, his real name is Nolan Oliver T. Lochlainn (or Nol for short, only his family call him that)

Yeong-Gi is Kousuke's half brother and is always going through it! The people surrounding him are so toxic except the few people in his corner like his grandma and his friends. He is apart of a distinguished family and although he doesn't like to, he has to attend social events his family puts on to keep up appearances.
I also think he's cute hehe (´ ω `♡)

Videogame Characters

【 Sans 】

Series: Undertale / Deltarune
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: They sell his jacket as official merch! I have it in a size too big lol, it's very comfy

Sans is just the local lazy skeleton that tells bad jokes. He often accompanies his younger brother, Papyrus, on his human catching shenanigans. He's also very powerful and intelligent although he may not look like it.

Idk how to explain the collective phenomenon that occurred in 2015 when Sans appeared. I just like the funny skeleton man. He's kind of a comfort character for me (´･ᴗ･ ` )

【 Maru 】

Series: Stardew Valley
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: She is the first character I romanced in Stardew Valley, I always gave her loved gifts and we got married during the start of the second year

Maru is a tinkerer, part-time nurse, space enjoyer, and lover of nature science. She's often found helping her dad with something science-related or building something in her room. She's really sweet and her family is too. Imo the best bacholerette option in Stardew Valley. She also gives you bombs sometimes as a gift when you get married. It was so funny the first time that happened to me.

【 Kotone Shiomi 】

Series: Persona 3
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: I've only played P3 as Kotone

Kotone is the MC of all time. I had a great time playing as her in Persona 3. Her friendships and interactions were unique to her experiences which I liked about the game. Also you get to learn about Shinjiro, another character I like in P3. You don't have him as a social link as Makoto and tbh, I think it's worth it lol.

【 Aigis 】

Series: Persona 3
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: My favorite toaster, is a highly skilled weapon in disguise

Aigis is one of my favorite characters in Persona next to Rio, Kotone, and Shinji. Aigis is a machine designed to fight against shadows. Of course since she's a robot, she acts like one in the beginning of the game. But as time goes on, she develops her own humanity through building connections with others. She deserves the world man. Actually best girl.

【 Leah 】

Series: Stardew Valley
Heart Rating: A
Fun Fact: She is an artist and wood is her favorite medium to work with

Leah is such a cutie. She's very in touch with nature and she loves things foraged from the wild as gifts. She moved from the city to Stardew Valley to live a quieter life. She's just a nice character all around. I love the friendship cutscenes she has. They're so nice (´ ω `♡)

【 Alex 】

Series: Stardew Valley
Heart Rating: B
Fun Fact: He wants to be a pro football player

This goober is the kinda jerky jock turned sweet once you get to know him. He's such a sweetheart though (´｡• ᵕ •｡`) ♡ He likes to stay fit and he liked eggs! (a lot) Like he will eat one whole if you give it to him lol. I need to see more of hit cutscenes so I can learn more about him tbh.

【 Ln'eta 】

Series: Sucker for Love: First Date
Heart Rating: B
Fun Fact: She's an eldritch horror and her dreams are their own individual realities!

Ln'eta is a Cthulu esc eldritch horror that you can date in Sucker for Love. She's my favorite next to Rhok'zan ♡＼(￣▽￣)／♡ I like them both hehe. Can you tell I like monster characters lmao In the game, you do tasks / perform rituals to prepare for your date, set up a nice scenery for her, or bring on the end of your universe :D I really had fun with the game. I definitely recommend playing it if you like monster girls like me o(>ω<)o

Misc. Characters

【 Eyeless Jack 】

Series: Creepypasta
Heart Rating: S
Fun Fact: He's very skilled in what he does, which is essentially surgery without you knowing

If you don't know Eyeless Jack, he's a creepypasta that goes around stealing people's kidneys. Usually in their sleep without them knowing. There's also some deeper lore about him being used for a sacrifice but it failed so he's like.. some kind of non-human thing with a thirst for blood. Yknow, just your average day.

He's a long time favorite of mine (♡˙︶˙♡) He is also a part of another phenomenon when everyone started simping for creepypastas, knowing good and well if you encountered these character, it would be over lol.
